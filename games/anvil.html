(Written in 2019) Anvil is probably my most impressive game. In it, you try to dodge falling anvils, and collect golden anvils to gain health. It was made with Adobe Flash.

Anvil has two dificulties, normal and hard. You beat the game by completing it (50/50) on hard mode.

good luck.
