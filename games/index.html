Interactives

I've made some Interactive in-browser things. Mostly games, but I might put other things. I don't trust any of these to work on mobile, but you can try your luck if you want. Have some fun with them if you want to.

Clicky 3 is a gold mining Idle game.

Anvil is probably my most impressive game. In it, you try to dodge falling anvils, and collect golden anvils to gain health. It was made with Adobe Flash.


Away is a text-adventury thing where you hear a knock on your door. It isn't very impressive, But it's fun for a few minutes. See if you can get all 6 endings. It was made with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
